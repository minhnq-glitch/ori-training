1. Gioi thieu he thong ORI

Clevai ORI System la he thong quan ly lop hoc thu (Orientation) truc tuyen, thay the quy trinh thu cong tren Google Sheet. He thong tu dong hoa 5 giai doan: Lap ke hoach → Book HV → Xep GV → Day & Danh gia → Bao cao.

Phien ban hien tai: v6.0.0 — Ho tro 7 san pham, 3 he thong (SOW, TEW, SCS), giao dien Clevai Warm Corporate.

2. So sanh: Quy trinh CU vs MOI

Quy trinh CU (Google Sheet)

  • Book HV thu cong tren GSheet, khong kiem tra trung/day
  • SO xep GV qua Zalo, khong co log
  • Tao Meet link thu cong bang App Script
  • GV danh gia qua Google Form (khong validate)
  • Report gui thu cong qua Zalo (screenshot)
  • Khong co Dashboard, khong co KPI real-time
  • Khong co bao cao Sale rieng
  • De sai: book trung, slot day, quen GV

Quy trinh MOI (ORI System)

  • SOW: Book online, tu dong kiem tra capacity + trung
  • Auto-match Reg4 + GV tu xac nhan tren TEW
  • Gen Meet 1 click cho tat ca lop du dieu kien
  • GV danh gia tren TEW Live-ORI (mandatory fields)
  • Report PDF tu dong, HV xem tren OSW
  • Dashboard real-time: 5 KPI, bieu do, fill rate
  • MOI: Sale Management — bao cao hieu suat Sale
  • Full audit log, khong the "lach" he thong

12. Logic he thong khi bam [Book Session]

  1. Validate inputs: Opportunity ID da xac minh, ten/SDT da co, PT/GG/DFDL/Date/Slot da chon, master data ton tai trong DB
  2. Business rules: Block trung (cung HV cung ngay+slot) | Capacity check (registered < max_per_class) | Plan check (VH ACTIVE plan cho PT+thang) | Deadline check (3 ngay + 16:30, co tru booking_override_until)
  3. Tao du lieu: OBD (status=BOOKED) → tim/tao CLAG → tao ULC → tao Student POD (VALIDORI) + CUI + PodClag + CLAG_ULC
  4. Ket qua: Thanh cong → toast xanh, form reset | Loi → toast do + ly do cu the

23. Logic he thong sau khi Submit danh gia

  1. Validate: Kiem tra tat ca truong bat buoc (*) da dien → thieu → toast do "Field is required"
  2. Gui API: POST /ori/evaluation → luu eval_data (JSON) vao OBD
  3. Thanh cong: Toast xanh "Evaluation submitted successfully", popup dong, danh sach reload
  4. Nut doi: [Evaluate] (xanh) → [Edit Evaluation] (vang). GV co the vao lai de sua.
  5. Auto-commit 3:00 AM: Cron commit tat ca eval_data → tao CuieDetails (AF-FN-RCM) → pwr-worker tao Report PDF → upload S3 → ghi report_link vao OBD
  6. Sau commit: Nut doi thanh "Report generated" (xanh nhat, disabled). Report link hien tren SOW > Classes.
Deadline danh gia: Truoc 3:00 AM ngay hom sau. Sau 3AM, eval da commit → khong sua duoc.
